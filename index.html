COPD Management Platform

The Sree Platform is an intelligent system comprising hardware and software for the management and prevention of Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD).


The platform provides easy readout and reporting, a community for socialising with experts and other people managing COPD, a doctor - both human and AI-backed, providing prompt responses to COPD management queries, interactive digital consultations and much more still.

Employee Wellness Platform

The Sree Employee Wellness platform enables physiological tracking and provides a comprehensive Physio interface for visualising health insights and reports. Additionally, it facilitates online therapy sessions through the Talk interface for pre-booked and on-demand digital consultations.

Physio

Physical Health Management

  • Heart Rate
  • SpO2
  • Heart Rate Variability
  • Sedentary time

As an employer, the platform provides you with regular anonymised reports to help you identify common issues among all employees, departments or teams, in order to address such issues and increase workplace satisfaction and productivity.

Talk

Mental Health Management

  • Digital therapy sessions with mental health experts
  • Mood assessment

Our Talk system allows you to select and talk to appropriate mental health experts, as well as to keep track of your mood, help reduce stress and optimise workplace engagement.

Integrated Report

Employer ROI

  • Identifying common employee concerns
  • Reporting on overall and department specific workplace satisfaction

With Physio, Sree monitors your blood oxygen, heart rate, heart rate variability and sedentary levels for anomalies to aid in the early detection of cardiovascular and respiratory diseases in the workplace.
